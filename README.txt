Project Light

Setting: You are trapped in a jungle that is in the middle of a warzone. Missiles are being fired in all directions and you are caught in the cross-fire. Animals are running rampant trying to save themselves. Your only hope for survival is to climb to the highest point in the jungle and call for help.

Objective: To reach the ball of light in motion at the highest point in every level while avoiding the numerous obstacles that may come your way.
